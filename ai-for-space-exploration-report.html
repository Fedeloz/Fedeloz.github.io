Session Report

AI for Space Exploration IEEE CAI 2026 special session in Granada, Spain.

A short report on the inaugural special session organized with NASA Ames at the IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence (CAI 2026).

May 10, 2026 · 11:30-13:30 · Aula 109 · University of Granada

Quick Facts

Event details

  • Conference

    IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence (IEEE CAI 2026).

  • Date and time

    May 10, 2026, from 11:30 to 13:30.

  • Venue

    Aula 109, Escuela Tecnica Superior de Ingenieria de Caminos, Canales y Puertos, University of Granada, Granada, Spain.

  • Organizers

    Ignacio G. Lopez-Francos, Andres Martinez, and Federico Lozano-Cuadra.

Coverage

Highlights

  • Scope

    The session focused on AI methods for space exploration, including federated learning for distributed space systems, multi-agent planetary mapping, high-fidelity 3D reconstruction, onboard science autonomy, and reinforcement learning for robotic locomotion in microgravity.

  • Outcome

    The inaugural edition brought together researchers from both sides of the Atlantic and helped strengthen collaboration between the AI and space communities.
